Present Tanse

Verbal Sentences (Kalimat Verbal):
(+) The train leaves at 6:00 a.m. (Kereta berangkat pukul 6 pagi.)
(-) The train does not leave at 6:00 a.m. (Kereta tidak berangkat pukul 6 pagi.)
(?) Does the train leave at 6:00 a.m.? (Apakah kereta berangkat pukul 6 pagi?)

Nominal Sentences (Kalimat Nominal):
(+) Cats hate water. (Kucing benci air.)
(-) I dont know her mother. (Aku tidak tahu ibunya.)
(?) Can it run quickly? (Apakah itu bisa berjalan dengan cepat?)


Past Tanse

example past tense:
(+) I was born in 2001.(Saya lahir pada tahun 2001.)
(-) I was not born in 2001.(Saya tidak lahir pada tahun 2001.),
(?) Were you born in 2001? (Apakah kamu lahir pada tahun 2001?)

(+)I went to Paris last year.( Saya pergi ke Paris tahun lalu.)
(-) I did not go to Paris last year.(Saya tidak pergi ke Paris tahun lalu.)
(?) Did you go to Paris last year?(Apakah kamu pergi ke Paris tahun lalu? )


Present Perfect Tanse

example Present Prfect tense :
(+) I have studied Japanese for two months. (Saya telah belajar bahasa Jepang selama dua bulan.)
(-) I have not studied Japanese for two months. (Saya belum ada dua bulan belajar bahasa Jepang.)
(?) Have you studied Japanese for two months? (Apakah kamu sudah belajar bahasa Jepang selama dua bulan?)


(+) They have been in Tangerang for two weeks. (Mereka sudah berada di Tangerang selama dua minggu.)
(-) They have not been in Tangerang for two weeks. (Mereka belum ada dua minggu di Tangerang.)
(?) Have they been in Tangerang for two weeks? (Apakah mereka sudah berada di Tangerang selama dua minggu?)


Present Present Continious Tanse

example Present Continious tense :
(+) Uncle John is sleeping in the living room.(Saat ini, dia sedang tidur di ruang keluarga. )
(-) They are not working today.(Mereka tidak bekerja hari ini. )
(?) Are you playing football at the beach?(Apakah kamu sedang bermain sepak bola di pantai?)


Present Present Continious Tanse

example Present Continious tense :
(+) I will follow him to go through the forest to find our lovely dog. (Saya akan mengikuti dia melewati hutan untuk mencari anjing kita yang manis.)
(-) They will not work tomorrow. (Mereka tidak akan bekerja besok.)
(?) Will you play football at the beach? (Apakah kamu akan bermain sepak bola di pantai?)
